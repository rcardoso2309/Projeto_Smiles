About Us

Smiles Dental is a large five surgery clinic conveniently located beside the Square in Tallaght and just one minute from the LUAS stop. We offer a full range of general dental services including emergency and cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, dental crowns and veneers. The team in Tallaght are dedicated to ensuring the best possible experience for every patient.
